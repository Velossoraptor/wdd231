Non Profit Membership

Price: Free

Ideal for Non Profit organizations, this membership includes the opportunity to be randomly featured on our home page, as well as featured on our directory. This membership also includes a newsletter featuring local events that vendors can set up at.

Bronze Membership

Price: $300 (billed annually)

Ideal for smaller organizations, this membership includes the opportunity to be featured on our directory, and advertized by the chamber. This membership also includes a newsletter featuring local events that vendors can set up at.

Silver Membership

Price: $400 (billed annually)

Ideal for mid sized organizations, this membership includes the opportunity to be featured on our directory, advertized by the chamber, and randomly featured on our home page. This membership also includes a newsletter featuring local events that vendors can set up at, as well as local business conference dates.

Gold Membership

Price: $550 (billed annually)

Ideal for mid-large sized organizations, this membership includes the opportunity to be featured on our directory, advertized by the chamber, and randomly featured on our home page. This membership also includes a newsletter featuring local events that vendors can set up at, local business conference dates, and a 15% discount on booking fees through the chamber. Gold members are also invited to attend the bi-annual business training events hosted by the chamber, which the 15% discount is applied to.
